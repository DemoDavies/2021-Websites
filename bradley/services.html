Services

We provide lifting solutions for projects of all sizes; whether it be a short-term requirement or a major contract, Hoistr can adapt to meet your needs. Our large fleet of cranes ranging from 5t to 750t, reinforce our capacity and capability to provide innovative lifting solutions.

The Hoistr fleet consists of 125+ cranes, 55+ travel towers and specialized access equipment items; the largest fleet of its kind in Australia.
