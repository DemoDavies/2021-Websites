About Us
CEO of Pursuit Art - Justin Park
Established Since Open since 2017, Pursuit Art provides a warm and welcoming space to aspiring artists, while offering them a chance to gain popularity and influence by displaying their works in our store.

Our Goal Pursuit Art aims to provide amazing service to all customers along with fantastic products and resources. As Pursuit Art is located in Shellharbour, NSW, Australia, there is always quick and easy access to the nearest airports and harbours for optimal delivery of quality goods for customers. This ensures that any orders or items are always available or quickly obtained for the best customer experience.

Our Amazing Achievements In our short time as an established company, we have won numerous awards, such as “Best Art Products” or “Best Customer Satisfaction”. Furthermore, for the past two years Pursuit Art has been awarded by the community with the title of “Most Outstanding Local Small Business”.

Given our incredible achievements, we understand that this is simply not only our own. One of our personal artists that frequently utilises our studio services and supplies won the $100,000 first prize in the 2020 edition of the Hadley’s Art Prize competition.
